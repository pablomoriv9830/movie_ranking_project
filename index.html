Best Movies According to Pablo

My top 3 movies of all-time

1. Shutter Island

This thriller is about an island that has a psychiatric hospital and two detectives have to investigate a disappearance there.

2. Howl's Moving Castle

I think this is the best movie from Hayao Miyazaki, the animation is perfect!

3. Gladiator

This movie is about the story of a Roman general, Maximus, who is betrayed by the emperor and forced to become a slave and gladiator.
